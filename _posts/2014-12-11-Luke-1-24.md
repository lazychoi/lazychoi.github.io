Meanwhile, the people were waiting for Zechariah and wondering why he stayed so long in the temple. When he came out, he could not speak to them. They realized he had seen a vision in the temple, for he kept making signs to them but remained unable to speak. When the time of service was completed, he returned home. 
**Aftr this his wife Elizabeth became pregnant and for five months she remained in seclusion[^1].**

부인 엘리자벳이 임신은 한다. 그리고 다섯 달 동안 사람들을 만나지 않고 지낸다. 

[^1]: seclusion: in a quiet place away from other people.

#### 성경 번역

그 뒤에 그의 아내 엘리사벳이 잉태하였다. 엘리사벳은 다섯 달 동안 숨어 지내며 이렇게 말하였다.

그 뒤에 그의 아내 엘리사벳은 아기를 가지게 되어 다섯 달 동안 들어앉아 있으면서
